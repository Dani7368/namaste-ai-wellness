Six Distinct Disciplines

The Practices

"Whether you come to sweat, to soften, or simply to breathe — there is a practice here that was made for the version of you that exists today."

01

Vinyasa Flow

All Levels · Breath-Led Movement

Vinyasa is the art of linking breath with movement — a flowing meditation that creates internal heat and lasting clarity. Each class is uniquely sequenced, meaning no two sessions are ever the same. Expect to move fluidly, breathe deeply, and leave lighter than you arrived.

60 – 90 min All levels Mat required

02

Yoga for
Beginners

Beginner · Foundational

The perfect first step. This class introduces essential postures, proper alignment, and the fundamentals of conscious breathing in a warm, unhurried environment. There is no expectation here — only presence, patience, and the willingness to begin.

60 min Beginners Props provided

03

Power Yoga

Intermediate · Advanced · High Intensity

Dynamic, athletic, and deeply transformative. Power Yoga challenges your strength, endurance, and mental fortitude through vigorous sequences that build muscular resilience and unwavering focus. Prepare to be tested — and to exceed your own expectations.

60 – 75 min Intermediate+ High intensity
Book this class →
04

Ashtanga
Yoga

All Levels · Structured Series

Rooted in ancient tradition, Ashtanga is a precise, sequential practice that links every posture to a specific breath and gaze point. As the series becomes familiar, the practice goes inward — revealing stillness within the storm of movement.

75 – 90 min All levels Mysore available
Book this class →
05

Pilates

All Levels · Core-Centred

Pilates is the art of intelligent movement — precise, controlled, and deeply effective. Each session targets the deep stabilising muscles of the core, improving posture, flexibility, and body awareness. The results are felt long after you leave the studio.

60 min All levels Mat & reformer
Book this class →
06

Flexibility

All Levels · Mobility & Release

A deeply nourishing practice dedicated to lengthening and releasing tension throughout the entire body. Using a blend of passive stretching, active mobility work, and breath-led surrender, this class will unlock a range of movement you didn't know you had.

60 min All levels Blocks & straps
